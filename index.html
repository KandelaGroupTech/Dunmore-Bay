Services

Full-Spectrum Deal Execution

From first conversation to final signatures, Dunmore Bay Advisors provides the advisory firepower to move transactions forward efficiently and with confidence.

Deal Structuring & Strategy

Define the optimal transaction structure — whether asset vs. share deal, earn-out mechanics, rollover equity, or staged acquisition — to maximise value and minimise risk for all parties.

Due Diligence Management

Coordinate and synthesise financial, legal, commercial, and operational due diligence workstreams. Identify deal risks early and translate findings into actionable negotiation leverage.

Negotiation & SPA Advisory

Lead commercial negotiations on price, terms, representations & warranties, indemnities, and closing conditions. Bridge the gap between legal language and business reality.

Financial Modelling & Valuation

Build robust transaction models — LBO, DCF, comparable company and precedent transaction analyses — to ground negotiations in data-driven valuation frameworks.

Process Management

Run disciplined buy-side or sell-side processes: manage data rooms, coordinate advisors, set bidder timelines, and drive momentum to keep transactions on track to close.

Post-Merger Integration Support

Protect deal value post-closing with integration planning, Day 1 readiness, synergy tracking, and executive alignment to ensure the deal thesis is realised in practice.
